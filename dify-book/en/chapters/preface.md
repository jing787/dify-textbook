# Preface

Over the past few years, generative AI has changed everything. ChatGPT made it possible for anyone to talk to AI — but chatting is just the tip of the iceberg.

The real question is: can you get AI to follow a process you design and actually get the work done?

That's exactly what Dify does. Whether you can code or not, you can build AI applications on the platform — from simple Q&A bots to multi-step workflows.

## How to Use This Book

See results in 5 minutes, get up and running in 30, and master a real scenario in 3 hours. No fluff — you'll get hands-on from Chapter 1.

## Cast of Characters

The protagonist is **Lynn**, an operations manager at an e-commerce company.

You'll follow Lynn as she uses Dify to solve three real business pain points:

1. **Customer service** → From Chatbot to Chatflow, building a smart CS agent with memory
2. **Weekly ops report** → Automatically analyzing data and generating reports
3. **Marketing content** → Batch-producing content across multiple platforms

Along the way, you'll cover Dify's core capabilities — building, orchestrating, publishing, and monitoring — taking an AI application from zero to production.

Finally, David suddenly drops a request: "Build a customer churn early-warning system." By then, you'll find that everything you've learned comes together.
