# Chapter 1: From Chatbot to AI Workflow

> Before jumping in, take a few minutes to understand what Dify actually is and how it differs from something like ChatGPT.

## What ChatGPT Changed

In late 2022, ChatGPT burst onto the scene and showed the world the power of large language models. In just a few years, AI capabilities have advanced dramatically — not only can it hold conversations, but it can write, use tools, and even operate computers.

But for businesses, there's a huge gap between "it can do it" and "it does it well."

Getting AI to answer a single question is easy. Getting it to reliably handle thousands of questions every day — following your business rules, based on your data — that takes more than a better prompt. You need an entire system.

Someone has to manage the knowledge base. Someone has to design the workflows. Business systems need to be connected. Performance needs to be monitored. When something goes wrong, you need to iterate.

A single chat window can't handle all of that.

## From "Conversation" to "Application"

The real business value isn't in chatting with AI — it's in getting AI to follow a process you've designed and reliably get the job done.

That's the difference between a "conversation" and an "application."

What does an AI application look like?

1. **Clear inputs and outputs**: Not an aimless chat, but a defined "give it this, get back that"
2. **A fixed processing flow**: Not random answers every time, but execution following steps you've designed
3. **External data integration**: Not just relying on the model's training data, but querying your documents, databases, and APIs
4. **Callable by other systems**: Not just a human clicking buttons in a UI, but accessible via API to plug into existing business systems

That's exactly what Dify does.
